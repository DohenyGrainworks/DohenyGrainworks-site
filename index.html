About Doheny Grainworks

Craftsmanship Across Generations.

Doheny Grainworks is a family-owned business led by a grandfather-grandson team in Connecticut. With a passion for precision and legacy, we create personalized wooden pieces that celebrate life’s milestones.

Co-founded by William L. Doheny IV and William Doheny Jr., Doheny Grainworks blends timeless woodworking traditions with cutting-edge CNC technology. Every piece we create—whether for a nursery, a wedding, or a boardroom—is designed to bring lasting meaning and value to the people and spaces it touches.

Our goal is simple: build things that matter—and build them to last.
